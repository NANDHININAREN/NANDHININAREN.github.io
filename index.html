Car Battery Charger

This is a simple car battery charger circuit with indication. The battery is charged from a 230V, 50Hz AC mains supply. This AC voltage is rectified and filtered to obtain an unregulated DC voltage used to charge the battery through a relay. This battery voltage is constantly monitored by a feedback circuitry compromised of a potential divider, a diode and a transistor.
